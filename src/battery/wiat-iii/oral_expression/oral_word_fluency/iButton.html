Administration

Start:
Grades PK-12+: Item 1

Timing

Allow 60 seconds for each item. Begin timing after the last word of instructions.

Recording

Don't Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of "I don't know" as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Repetition (R): Used to record when the examinee indicates a need for repetition

Scoring

No credit is given the examples given in the directions (e.g., bear or goat for Item 1, and red or lavender for Item 2)
 
Responses must be in English to receive credit.
 
Assign credit or no credit according to the rules in the WIAT-III Examiner's Manual.

Refer to the WIAT-III Examiner's Manual for additional information.
