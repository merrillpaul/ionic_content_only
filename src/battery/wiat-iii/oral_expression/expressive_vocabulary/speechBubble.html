Instructions

Examinee responds to an item with more than one word
Say, Tell me one word.
Examinee provides an ambiguous response, related synonym, or slang term
Query by saying, Tell me another word. 
Examinee has been performing poorly and tends to consider responses for long periods without any benefit
Encourage him or her to respond after 30 seconds by saying, Do you have an answer? If the examinee does not respond, move on to the next item by saying, Let’s try another one.
Examinee requests repetition
Item prompts may be repeated as necessary.
