Instructions

Child pauses or struggles to read a word
If the child has not read the word after 5 seconds, read the word aloud and record a G (given) above the word. Refer to the timer to assess whether 5 seconds have passed.

Child skips a line
Direct the examinee to the correct line, but do not stop timing.

Child is reluctant to respond to an item
Prompt him or her by saying, Just try your best.
