To begin administration, give the examinee a pencil without an eraser and say, You will be using a pencil without an eraser, so if you make a mistake, just cross it out. Open the Response Booklet to Alphabet Writing Fluency and hand it to the examinee.

Say, I want you to write as many letters of the alphabet as you can. Write them as quickly as you can. The letter a has been done for you. Point to the letter a in the Response Booklet and say, Next to the letter a, write other letters you know. Do you have any questions? 

When the examinee is ready, say, Begin. 

At the end of 30 seconds, say Stop.
