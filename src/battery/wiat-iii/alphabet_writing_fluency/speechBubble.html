Instructions

Examinee begins writing the same letters in both uppercase and lowercase (or in both print and cursive)
Say, Write each letter only once. 

Examinee does not understand the task
Say, You can write the letters of the alphabet in order, starting with a-b-c, or you can write any letters you know, like the letters in your name.

Examinee indicates need for repetition
Instructions may be repeated as needed.
