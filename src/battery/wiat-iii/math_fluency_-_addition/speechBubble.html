Instructions

Examinee's written response is not legible
Wait until the last item is administered and then ask the student to read the illegible character by saying, I cannot read what this says, before asking the student to clarify a written response.

Examinee does not complete the problems in the order specified
Do not stop timing, but immediately redirect the student to the appropriate item order. The items must be completed in this order.

Examinee indicates need for repetition
Instructions may be repeated as needed.
