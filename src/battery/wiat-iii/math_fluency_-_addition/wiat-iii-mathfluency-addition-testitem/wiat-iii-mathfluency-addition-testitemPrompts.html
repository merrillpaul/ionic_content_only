To begin administration, give the examinee a pencil without an eraser and say, You will be using a pencil without an eraser, so if you make a mistake, just cross it out.

Open the Response Booklet to Math Fluency - Addition, with both pages facing the examinee, and say, I want you to write the answers to as many math problems as you can in one minute. 

Point to the first item and say, Start here and work the problems in this order.

Demonstrate by sweeping your finger from left to right across the first row and then across the next row of the first page. Encourage the examinee to proceed by row, completing the left page first, then the right page.

Say, If you come to a problem that you don’t know, just skip it. Do you have any questions?

When the examinee is ready, point to the first item and say, Begin.

If the student does not work the problems in the order specified, immediately redirect the student to the appropriate item order, but do not stop timing.

At the end of 60 seconds say, Stop.
