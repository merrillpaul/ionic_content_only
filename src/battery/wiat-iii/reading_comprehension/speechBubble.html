Instructions

Examinee asks for reading or vocabulary assistance
Say, I cannot help you with any of the words. Just do the best you can. Do not read any words for the student.

In response to an item, the examinee points to the passage without telling about it or vaguely refers to part of the passage (e.g., "the middle")
Say, Tell me what it says.

Examinee has been performing poorly and tends to consider responses for long periods without any benefit
Encourage him or her to respond after 30 seconds by saying, Do you have an answer? If the examinee does not respond, move on to the next item by saying, Let’s try another one.
Examinee's response is unclear or followed by a Q in the sample responses
Say, What do you mean? or Tell me more.
Examinee indicates need for repetition
Item prompts may be repeated as needed.
