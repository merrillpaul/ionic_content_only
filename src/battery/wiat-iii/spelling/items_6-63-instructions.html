For grades K-1 only: Say, Now let's try writing some words. I will say a word, then use it in a sentence, and say the word again. Listen to the sentence so you know what word to write. 

For grades 2-12 only: Say, I want you to write some words. I will say a word, then use it in a sentence, and say the word again. Listen to the sentence so you know what word to write.
