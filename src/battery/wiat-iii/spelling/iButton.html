Materials

Response Booklet
Pencil without eraser

Administration

Prior to administration, listen to the Spelling audio track in Central and refer to the Examiner's Manual to familiarize yourself with the acceptable pronunciation(s) of the vocabulary words.

Read item prompts clearly and at a conversational pace. Do not pronounce spelling words slowly or in parts. Score responses during administration, without interrupting or distracting the examinee, in order to apply reverse and discontinue rules as prompted by the system.

Start
Grades K-1: Item 1
Grade 2: Item 7
Grade 3: Item 9
Grades 4-8: Item 16
Grades 9-12+: Item 20

Reverse
Grades 2-12+: If score of 0 on any of the first 3 items given, administer preceding items in reverse order until 3 consecutive scores of 1. 

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0

Recording

Don't Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of "I don't know" as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Skills Analysis: Used to record incorrect responses verbatim and categorize errors by type

Scoring

1 point is awarded for each correct response and 0 points awarded for each incorrect, DK, or NR response. 

For Grades K-1 only: Do not penalize for letter reversals on any item administered, except for when the letter reversal forms a different letter.

Refer to the WIAT-III Examiner's Manual for additional information.
