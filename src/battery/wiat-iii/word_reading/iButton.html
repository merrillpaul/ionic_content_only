Materials

Client Device (Flat; Portrait View)

Administration

Prior to administration, listen to the Word Reading audio track in Central and refer to the Examiner's Manual for scoring assistance.

Start
Grades 1-12+: Item 1

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0

Timing

Begin timing after reading the last word of instructions. Record the item number completed at 30 seconds. (Continue to administer items until the Discontinue Rule is met). 

Recording

Don't Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of "I don't know" as his or her only answer

Prompt (P): Used to record when a subtest-specific prompt is provided

More Than Three Seconds (>3"): Used to record when the examinee takes longer than 3 seconds to respond 

Self-Correct (SC): Used to record when the examinee self-corrects 

Readministered Item (!): Used to record all items that should be readministered because the examinee did not say the word clearly

Skills Analysis: Used to record incorrect responses verbatim and categorize errors by type

Scoring

Pronunciation must be fluent to receive credit. 

1 point is awarded for each correct response and 0 points awarded for each incorrect or DK response. 

Record the item completed at 30 seconds.

Refer to the WIAT-III Examiner's Manual for additional information.
