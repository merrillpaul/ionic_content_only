Instructions

Examinee does not say a word clearly
Flag the item using the (!) button and continue administration. After the final item is administered, point to the row containing the item in question on the Client Device and say, I did not hear you clearly. Please read these words again, and score only the target word. If the word is still unclear, score the item 0.

Examinee's pronunciation is slow or choppy
Prompt the examinee once per item by saying, Say it all together. If the next attempt is not fluent, score the item 0 and say, Try the next one.

A word or row is skipped
Redirect the examinee to the appropriate place and make a note of this.

Multiple attempts are made to read an item
Score only the last attempt.

Examinee indicates need for repetition
Instructions may be repeated as needed.
