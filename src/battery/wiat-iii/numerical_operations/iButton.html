Administration

It is recommended that you either (a) allow the examinee to complete as many items as possible and then apply reverse and discontinue rules after administration; or (b) surreptitiously score responses without distracting the examinee and apply reverse and discontinue rules during administration.

The examinee is not permitted to use a calculator.

Start
Grades K-1: Item 1
Grades 2-4: Item 14
Grades 5-12+: Item 18

Reverse
Grades 2-12+: If score of 0 on any of the first 3 items given, administer preceding items in reverse order until 3 consecutive scores of 1. 

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0

Recording

Don't Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of "I don't know" as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Scoring

1 point is awarded for each correct response and 0 points awarded for each incorrect or DK response. 

Refer to the WIAT-III Examiner's Manual for additional information.
