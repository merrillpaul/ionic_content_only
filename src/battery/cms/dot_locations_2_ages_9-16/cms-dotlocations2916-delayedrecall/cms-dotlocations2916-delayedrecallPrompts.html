Place the 4 x 4 Response Grid and 8 Response Chips in front of the examinee. Be sure that the orientation arrows on the Response Grid are pointing toward the Examiner Device.

Say, Do you remember the box with blue dots inside it that I showed you earlier three times in a row? Put the chips (point to the Response Chips) on the grid (point to the Response Grid) in the same places you saw the blue dots in the box. Ready? Take the chips and show me where the blue dots were.

If the examinee does not understand the directions, it is permissible to repeat them, paraphrasing as necessary. Allow the examinee ample time to respond. If the examinee does not place all the Response Chips on the Response Grid, say, Place all the chips on the grid. If you can't remember where to put them, make your best guess.

Record the examinee's response.
