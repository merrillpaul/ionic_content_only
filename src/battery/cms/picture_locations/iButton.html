Administration

Start
Ages 5-16: Identification Item

Discontinue
Do not discontinue.

Stop
Ages 5-8: After Item 10

Timing

Present the item stimulus for 2 seconds using the on-screen timer.

Recording

Don't Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of "I don't know" as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Prompt (P): Used to record when any subtest-specific prompt is provided

Repetition (R): Used to record when the examinee indicates a need for repetition 

Scoring

Responses are scored by entering the chips from the examiner's point of view.

Items 1 and 2: Score 0 or 1 points.
Items 3 and 4: Score 0-2 points.
Items 5 and 6: Score 0-3 points.
Items 7 and 8: Score 0-4 points.
Items 9 and 10: Score 0-5 points.
Items 11 and 12: Score 0-6 points.
Items 13 and 14: Score 0-7 points.
Items 15 and 16: Score 0-8 points.

Refer to the CMS Manual for additional information.
