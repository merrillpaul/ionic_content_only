Instructions

Examinee does not place all the chips on the Response Grid
Say, Place all the chips on the grid. If you can't remember where to put them, make your best guess.

Examinee refuses to respond to an item or hesitates while responding
Say, Just try it once more or Try it just a little longer or I think you can do it.

Examinee asks for help
Say, I want to see how well you can do it yourself.

Examinee indicates need for repetition
Where useful, repeat a question after giving encouragement.
