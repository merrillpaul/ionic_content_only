Then the box will disappear from the screen, and I want you to put the chips (point to the Response Chips) on this grid (point to the Response Grid) in the same places that you saw the dots in the box. Ready? Let's try it. Look closely.

Show stimulus.

After the Client Device goes blank, say, Now take the chips and show me where the dots were. (Point to the Response Chips and Response Grid.)

Allow the examinee ample time to respond. After recording the chip placement, remove the chips from the Response Grid and place them in front of the examinee.
