Place the 4 x 4 Response Grid and 8 Response Chips in front of the examinee.

Say, I'm going to show you a screen with blue dots on it. I want you to look very carefully at the dots and remember where they are on the screen. Then the dots will disappear from the screen, and I want you to put the chips (point to the Response Chips) on this grid (point to the Response Grid) in the same places that you saw the dots on the screen. Ready? Let's try it. Look closely.

After the screen clears, say, Now take the chips and show me where the dots were. (Point to the Response Chips and Response Grid.)

Allow the examinee ample time to respond. After recording the chip placement, remove the chips from the Response Grid and place them in front of the examinee.
