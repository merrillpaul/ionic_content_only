Discontinue

Recording

Don't Know (DK): Used to record when the child provides an answer of "I don't know" as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the child does not respond

Prompt Administered (P): Used to record when any subtest-specific prompt is provided 

Scoring

All items: Total the number of correctly placed chips for each Item.

Items 1 and 2: Score 0 or 1 points.
Items 3 and 4: Score 0, 1, or 2 points.
Items 5 and 6: Score 0-3 points.
Items 7 and 8: Score 0-4 points.
Items 9 and 10: Score 0-5 points.
Items 11 and 12: Score 0-6 points.
Items 13 and 14: Score 0-7 points.
Items 15 and 16: Score 0-8 points.

Picture Locations Maximum Total Raw Score, Ages 5-8: 30 points
Picture Locations Maximum Total Raw Score, Ages 9-16: 72 points
