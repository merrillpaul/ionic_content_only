Instructions
Child requests a pencil
The child may not use a pencil or paper for any item, but do not discourage the child from writing on the table with his or her finger.
Child spontaneously writes on the table with his or her finger
Do not discourage the child from writing on the table with his or her finger.
Child does not indicate his or her choice by either touching, pointing to, or saying the number of the selected response (e.g., names the shapes)
Say, Show me.
Child gives multiple responses or self-corrects
Say, You (touched, pointed to, said) [insert child’s response], and you (touched, pointed to, said) [insert child’s response]. Which one did you mean? Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
