Instructions
Child asks you to repeat an item
Each item may be repeated as often as necessary, but do not alter the wording in any way.
Child’s response is unclear, vague, or followed by a Q in the sample responses
Say, What do you mean? or Tell me more about it (or some other neutral inquiry).
Child gives a verbal and a nonverbal response that are contradictory (e.g., says “Four” but holds up five fingers)
Say, Which one do you mean?
