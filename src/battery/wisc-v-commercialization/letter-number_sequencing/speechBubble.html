Instructions
Child begins to respond before you finish reading a trial
Present the remainder of the trial and allow the child to respond. Award appropriate credit for the response and then say, Wait until I stop before you start. 
Child asks you to repeat a trial
Do not repeat any trial. Say, I can only say them one time. Just take your best guess.
Child provides multiple responses or self-corrects 
Say, You said [insert child’s response], and you said [insert child’s response]. Which one did you mean?
