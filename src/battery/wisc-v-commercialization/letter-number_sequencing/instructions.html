Qualifying Items

Items 1–10

Read each trial verbatim at the rate of one number or letter per second, dropping your voice inflection slightly on the last number or letter in the sequence. Pause to allow the child to respond.

Record the child's response verbatim, touch the appropriate score button, and swipe to the next page.

Note: For Items 3–10, the child receives credit if all of the numbers and letters are recalled in the correct sequence, even if the letters are recalled before the numbers.
