Administration

Start
Ages 6–16: Demonstration Item, Sample Item, then Item 1

Discontinue
After 45 seconds for each item

Timing

The time limit for each item is 45 seconds.

Accurate timing is essential. Begin timing after saying the last word of instruction.

Stop timing when the child completes the item, the time limit expires, or it is clear from the child’s words or gestures that he or she has finished.

Recording

Prompt Administered (P): Used to record when any subtest-specific prompt is provided

Scoring

Use the Cancellation Scoring Template to score the child’s responses.

Score 1 point for each correct response.

For each item, enter the total number of correct and incorrect responses in the appropriate boxes.

Refer to the WISC–V Administration and Scoring Manual for additional information.
