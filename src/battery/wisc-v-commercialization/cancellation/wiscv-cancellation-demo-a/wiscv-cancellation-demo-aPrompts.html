Place the Response Booklet in front of the child. Ensure the child sees only the row of animal targets and the demonstration and sample items on the cover page.

Point across the row of animal targets, from the child’s left to right, and say, Look at these. They are all animals.

Point to the demonstration item and say, Now look at these. There are animals and other things too. I will only mark the animals. I will not mark anything else (draw a single slash mark through each of the four targets).
