Instructions
Child does not indicate his or her choice by either touching, pointing to, or saying the numbers of the selected responses (e.g., describes the puzzle pieces)
Say, Show me.
Child asks if his or her responses must be provided in numerical order
Say, You do not have to choose them in order. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt. 
Child selects fewer than three response options for an item
Provide the following prompt as often as necessary: Choose three pieces to make the puzzle. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child selects more than three response options for an item, gives multiple responses, or self-corrects
Say, Which three pieces did you mean? Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child states that one or more pieces are incorrectly oriented relative to the completed puzzle
Say, You may have to turn a piece in your mind to make it fit. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
