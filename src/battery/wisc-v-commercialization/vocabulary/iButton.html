Materials

Client Device (Flat)

Administration

For Items 1–4, read each item verbatim and point to the pictures on the Client Device as instructed. Items 5–29 are verbal items that are read verbatim to the child. Remove the Client Device from the child’s view when administering verbal items.

Start
Ages 6–7: Item 1
Ages 8–11: Item 5
Ages 12–16: Item 9
Children suspected of having an intellectual disability or low cognitive ability should start with Item 1.

Reverse
If a child aged 8–16 does not obtain a perfect score on either of the first two items given, administer the preceding items in reverse order until the child obtains perfect scores on two consecutive items.

Discontinue
After 3 consecutive scores of 0

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the child provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only response

No Response (NR): Used to record when the child does not respond

Query Administered (Q): Used to record when any query is provided

Item Repetition (IR): Used to record when an item is repeated

†Teaching: Used to access corrective feedback for imperfect scores on Items 1, 5, 6, 9, & 10

*Query: Used to access specific queries for sample responses followed by an asterisk

Scoring

Items 1–4: Score 0 or 1 point.
Items 5–29: Score 0, 1, or 2 points.

Refer to the WISC–V Administration and Scoring Manual for additional information.
