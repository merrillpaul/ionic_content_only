Picture Items (Items 1–4)

Picture items are presented on the Examinee Tablet. Read each item verbatim to the child as you point to the picture on the child’s screen. Record the child’s response verbatim, touch the appropriate score button, and swipe to the next page.

Verbal Items (Items 5–31)

Remove the Examinee Tablet from the child’s view before administering verbal items

Read each item verbatim to the child. Record the child’s response verbatim, touch the appropriate score button, and swipe to the next page.

Detailed administration and scoring information can be accessed at any time during administration by touching the â“˜ button. For increased accessibility, verbatim prompts also can be accessed by touching the speech button.

Each item may be repeated as often as necessary, but do not alter the wording in any way. Touch IR if an item is repeated.

A popup message appears to alert you that the discontinue criterion has been met. Touch Discontinue if you are confident about the accuracy of the item scores you have assigned. If you are unsure about the accuracy of the item scores, touch Test Limits and swipe to the next page. When you are confident that the discontinue criterion has been met, touch the subtest discontinue hand button at the top of the examiner’s screen.

To begin administration, swipe to the next page. If necessary, select a start point and touch OK.
