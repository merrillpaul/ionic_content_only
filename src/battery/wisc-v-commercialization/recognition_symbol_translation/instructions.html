Read the response options verbatim to the child, but do not include the letter (i.e., A, B, C, or D) that identifies the response option.

Verbatim instructions may be shortened or eliminated when the child understands the task.

If the child selects the correct response, touch the symbol to turn it green.

Touching a green symbol a second time changes it back to white, indicating an incorrect response or no response.

If the child selects an incorrect response or does not respond, leave the symbol in its default state (i.e., white).

Touch the 0 Points button if the child selects an incorrect response, says he or she does not know the answer, or does not respond within approximately 30 seconds.

Touch DK if the child says he or she does not know the answer. Touch NR if the child does not respond within approximately 30 seconds.
