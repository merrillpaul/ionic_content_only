Client Device (Flat)

Administration

Administer Recognition Symbol Translation immediately after Delayed Symbol Translation if both subtests are given. If Delayed Symbol Translation was not administered, administration of Recognition Symbol Translation should begin approximately 20–30 minutes after the completion of Immediate Symbol Translation.

Start
Ages 6–16: Item 1

Discontinue
Discontinue at the same decision point as Immediate Symbol Translation. If the child did not discontinue on Immediate Symbol Translation, do not discontinue.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the child provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only response

No Response (NR): Used to record when the child does not respond

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point.

Refer to the WISC–V Administration and Scoring Manual for additional information.
