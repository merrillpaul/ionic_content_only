The symbols and their correct translations are displayed on the examiner’s screen.

It is imperative that accurate scoring occurs during administration. 

  • If the child translates the symbol correctly, touch the corresponding symbol to turn it green, indicating a correct translation.

  • Touching a green symbol a second time changes it back to white, indicating an incorrect translation or no response.

  • If the child translates the symbol incorrectly or does not respond, leave the symbol in its default state (i.e., white).

  • Touch the 0 Points button if the child is unable to correctly translate any symbol, says he or she does not know the answer, or the child does not respond within approximately 30 seconds.
