Say, Now look at these. Point to the and say, This one means HOUSE, this one (point to ) means BESIDE, this one (point to ) means UNDER, and this one (point to ) means SEE. Now you say them after me.

Point to each symbol as you translate it aloud and pause to allow the child to respond, HOUSE…BESIDE…UNDER…SEE. Immediately correct any translation errors. If necessary, encourage the child to respond.
