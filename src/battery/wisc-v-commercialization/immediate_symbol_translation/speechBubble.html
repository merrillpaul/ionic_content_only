Instructions
Child does not translate a symbol after approximately 5 seconds, or says he or she does not know the meaning of a symbol
Say, Go on to the next one.
Child gives multiple translations for a symbol or self-corrects
Point to the first symbol in the string and say, Start again from here.
Child begins to translate symbols from his or her right to left
Point to the first symbol in the string and say, Start again from here.
Child’s location in the symbol string during translation is unclear
Point to the first symbol in the string and say, Start again from here.
