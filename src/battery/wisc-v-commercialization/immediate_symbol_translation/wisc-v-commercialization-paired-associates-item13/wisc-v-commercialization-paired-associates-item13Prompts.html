Say, Now look at these. Point to the and say, This one means WE, this one (point to ) means SUN, this one (point to ) means WITH, and this one (point to ) means HAS or HAVE. Now you say them after me.

Point to each symbol as you translate it aloud and pause to allow the child to respond, WE…SUN…WITH…HAS or HAVE. Immediately correct any translation errors. If necessary, encourage the child to respond.
