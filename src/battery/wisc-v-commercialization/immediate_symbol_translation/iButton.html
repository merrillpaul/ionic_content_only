Client Device (Flat)

Administration

Start
Ages 6–16: Item 1

Discontinue
Discontinue if the child’s cumulative raw score is less than or equal to the specified value after administration of Item 6, Item 10, or Item 14. Children who do not meet the discontinue criterion after administration of Item 14 are administered all items (Items 1–21). 

Scoring

Record each correct response using the symbol buttons.

Refer to the WISC–V Administration and Scoring Manual for additional information.
