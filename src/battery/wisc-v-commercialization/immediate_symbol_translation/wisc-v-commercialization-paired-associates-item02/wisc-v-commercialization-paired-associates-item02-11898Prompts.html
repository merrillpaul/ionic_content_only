Say, Now look at these. Point to the and say, This one means DOG, and this one (point to ) means THE.

Point to the and say, This one means AND, and this one (point to ) means BOY. Now you say them after me.

Point to each symbol as you translate it aloud and pause to allow the child to respond, DOG…THE…AND…BOY. Immediately correct any translation errors. If necessary, encourage the child to respond.
