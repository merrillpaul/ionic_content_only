Instructions
Child asks what to do if they make a mistake
Say, That’s OK. Just keep working as fast as you can.
Child uses more than one finger or both hands to select responses
Say, You can only use one finger.
Child begins working the demonstration items
Say, I will show you how to do them first.
Child stops working or hesitates for more than 5 seconds before time expires
Say, Keep working as fast as you can until you see the check mark.
Child removes hands from screen between items
Say, Keep your hands near the screen to work as fast as you can.
Child repeatedly selects a response option in the same position (e.g., the first option on the left, the option in the middle) without considering the stimuli
Redirect the child to the task. If the child continues to repeatedly select a response option in the same position, discontinue administration of the subtest.
