Follow the instructions on the screen.

If the child’s screen becomes unresponsive due to secondary hand contact at any time during administration, say, Don’t let your hand touch the screen. Do not pause timing to provide this prompt. To record the event, first touch the List button, and then touch HC.

If the child finishes all of the test items on a page and does not touch the arrow or does not touch the arrow hard enough to advance the page, touch the arrow on the child’s screen and say, When you finish a page, touch the arrow and keep working as fast as you can. Do not pause timing to provide this prompt. To record the event, first touch the List button, and then touch AA.

It is essential that you watch the child during completion of test items in the event that a prompt is required or the child hesitates to advance to the next page by touching the arrow.
