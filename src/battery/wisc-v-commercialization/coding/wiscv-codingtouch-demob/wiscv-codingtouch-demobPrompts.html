Point across the shapes in the key at the top of the child’s screen and say, Look up here. Each box has a number in the top part (point across digits from 1 to 9) and a special mark in the bottom (point across symbols). Each number has its own special mark (point to 1 and its symbol, then 2 and its symbol).

Point to the demonstration item and say, Down here, the numbered box is empty. I will show you how to choose the right mark.

Point to the first demonstration item (2) and say, Here is a 2. The 2 has this mark (point to corresponding symbol in key). I find the mark down here (point to search group), and touch it (touch matching mark). Then I will see the next one.

Point to the second demonstration item (4) and say, Here is a 4. The 4 has this mark (point to corresponding symbol in key), so I touch it like this (touch matching mark).

Point to the third demonstration item (1) and say, Here is a 1. The 1 has this mark (point to corresponding symbol in key), so I touch it like this (touch matching mark).
