Instructions
Child begins to complete the test items before you finish the instructions
Say, Wait until I say “Go” to start.
Child makes a spontaneous correction
Do not discourage the child unless he or she does so repeatedly and it impedes performance.
Child omits an item or begins to complete a row in reverse order
Say, Do them in order. Don’t skip any. Point to the first omitted item and say, Do this one next. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child asks what to do if he or she makes a mistake
Say, That’s OK. Just keep working as fast as you can. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
