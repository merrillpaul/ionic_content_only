Point across the shapes in the key at the top of the child’s screen and say, Look at these shapes. Point to the mark inside of each shape as you say, Each shape has its own special mark.

Point to the demonstration item and say, Down here, this shape is empty. I will show you how to choose the right mark for this shape.

Point to the first demonstration item (circle) and say, Here is a circle. The circle has this mark (point to corresponding symbol in key). I find the mark down here (point to search group), and touch it (touch matching mark). Then I will see the next one.

Point to the second demonstration item (star) and say, Here is a star. The star has this mark (point to corresponding symbol in key). I find the mark down here (point to search group), and touch it (touch matching mark).
