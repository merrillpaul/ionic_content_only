Instructions
Child begins to mark the test items before you finish the instructions
Say, Wait until I say “Go” to start.
Child makes a spontaneous correction
Do not discourage the child unless he or she does so repeatedly and it impedes performance.
Child makes a mark by using anything other than a single slash mark
Point to the error and say, Draw one line to make each mark. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child marks a target symbol as a response to any item
Say, Make your marks over here (point across search group and NO box). Do not stop timing to provide this prompt. 
Child omits an item or begins to complete a row in reverse order
Say, Do them in order. Don’t skip any. Point to the first omitted item and say, Do this one next. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child asks what to do if he or she makes a mistake
Say, That’s OK. Just keep working as fast as you can. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt. 
Child forgets to turn the page or has difficulty turning the page
Turn the page for him or her and say, Keep working as fast as you can. Do not stop timing to turn the page or to provide this prompt.
