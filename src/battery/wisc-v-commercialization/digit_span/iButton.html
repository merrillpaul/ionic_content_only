Administration

Start
Forward

Ages 6–16: Item 1

Backward

Ages 6–16: Sample Item, then Item 1

Sequencing

Ages 6–7: Qualifying Item, Sample Items A & B, then Item 1
Ages 8–16: Sample Items A & B, then Item 1

Discontinue
Forward & Backward

Ages 6–16: After scores of 0 on both trials of an item

Sequencing

Ages 6–7: After an incorrect response to the Qualifying Item OR after scores of 0 on both trials of an item
Ages 8–16: After scores of 0 on both trials of an item

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the child provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only response

No Response (NR): Used to record when the child does not respond

Requested Repetition (RR): Used to record when the child asks for an item to be repeated

Self-Correction (SC): Used to record when the child self-corrects

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point for each trial.

Refer to the WISC–V Administration and Scoring Manual for additional information.
