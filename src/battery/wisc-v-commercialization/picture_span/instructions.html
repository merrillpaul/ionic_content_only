The correct response for each test item is listed on the examiner’s screen. For Items 1–4, the correct response is under the 1 Point button. For Items 5–27, the 2-point response, with the correct pictures in the correct sequence, is listed under the 2 Points button. When the child’s touch responses are properly registered, the selection order is recorded above the image on the examiner’s screen. Ensure that the child’s responses (i.e., the sequence of all selected pictures) are accurately reflected on the screen. Deselect and reselect pictures, as appropriate, to reflect the sequence of the child’s responses.

Touch the 0 Points button if the child says he or she does not know the answer, or the child does not respond within approximately 30 seconds.
