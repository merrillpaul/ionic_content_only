If the child makes a mistake on a sample item, correct the error immediately. Use the item to review use of the key. Continue to help the child, if necessary, until the child correctly completes the sample items. Use explanations such as You see, this is a 9. The 9 has this mark (point to 9 in key), so you should draw the same mark here (point to empty square).

Do not proceed with the test items until the child has correctly completed the sample items and understands the task. If it is clear that the child will not be able to understand the task with further instruction, discontinue the subtest by touching the subtest discontinue hand button at the top of the examiner’s screen.
