Touch the Start button on the stopwatch to start timing. If the child has not responded within approximately 30 seconds, prompt him or her by asking, Do you have an answer? Adjust the timing of this prompt and/or grant additional time if the child has established a pattern of providing delayed but correct responses as the item difficulty increases.

Stop timing when the child responds. If necessary (e.g., the child self-corrects after timing was stopped), touch Resume to continue timing until the child indicates his or her final response.

If the child provides multiple responses or self-corrects his or her initial response, ensure the intended response option is the only selected response on the examiner’s screen. Deselect any unintended response options.

Touch the 0 Points button if the child says he or she does not know the answer, or the child does not respond within approximately 30 seconds.
