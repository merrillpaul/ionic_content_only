Introduce the WISC–V Digital Edition by saying, We’ll be doing a lot of things today, like looking at pictures, answering questions, and working with blocks. Some things may be easy for you, but some may be hard. Just try your best.

To introduce the Block Design subtest, place two blocks in front of the child. Pick up one block and say, See these blocks? They are all alike. On some sides, they are all red (show red side); on some sides, all white (show white side); and on some sides, half red and half white (show red-and-white side).
