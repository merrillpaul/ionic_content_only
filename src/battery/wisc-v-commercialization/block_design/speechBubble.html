Instructions
Child hesitates or seems merely to be playing with the blocks
Say, Work as fast as you can. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child attempts to duplicate the side faces of the model
Point to the top faces of the blocks and say, Only the tops of the blocks need to be the same. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child rotates the Client Device
Do not allow the child to rotate the Client Device for any item.
Child attempts to construct the design on top of the Client Device
Point to the appropriate area next to the Client Device and say, Make yours over here.
Child does not correct a rotation of 30° or more within the time limit
Score 0. Correct only the first rotation error that occurs. Rotate the constructed design to the correct position and say, See, it goes this way.
