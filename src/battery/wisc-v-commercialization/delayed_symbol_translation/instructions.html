When instructed, the verbatim instructions may be shortened or eliminated when the child understands the task.

The symbols and their correct translations are displayed on the examiner’s screen.

  • If the child translates the symbol correctly, touch the symbol to turn it green, indicating a correct translation.

  • Touching a green symbol a second time will change it back to white, indicating an incorrect translation or no response.

  • If the child translates the symbol incorrectly or does not respond, leave the symbol in its default state (i.e., white).

  • Touch the 0 Points button if the child is unable to correctly translate any symbol, says he or she does not know the answer, or does not respond within approximately 30 seconds.
