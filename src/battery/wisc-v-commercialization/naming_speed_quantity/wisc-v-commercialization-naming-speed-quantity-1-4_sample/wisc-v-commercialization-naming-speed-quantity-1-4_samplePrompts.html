Say, Look at these pictures. Point to the appropriate pictures as you say, Each box has one, two, three, or four squares. When I say “Go,” name how many squares are in each box as fast as you can without making mistakes. Start here (point to first element in first row), go in order, and don’t skip any. When you finish this row, start the next one from here (point to first element in second row). Use your finger to keep your place, like this (run finger along elements in first and second rows, in order).

Are you ready?
