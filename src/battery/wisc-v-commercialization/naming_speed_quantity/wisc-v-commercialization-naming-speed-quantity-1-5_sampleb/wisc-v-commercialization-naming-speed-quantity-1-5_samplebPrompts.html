Say, Look at these pictures. Point to the appropriate boxes as you say, Each box has one, two, three, four, or five squares. When I say “Go,” name how many squares are in each box as fast as you can without making mistakes. Start here (point to first box in first row), go in order, and don’t skip any. When you finish this row, start the next one from here (point to first box in second row). Are you ready?

Explain further if necessary, then say, Go. Allow the child to complete the sample item.
