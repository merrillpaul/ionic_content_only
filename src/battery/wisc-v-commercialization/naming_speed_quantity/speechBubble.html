Instructions
Child aged 6–8 does not use finger tracking
Say, Use your finger to keep your place. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child begins naming the quantities before you finish the instructions
Say, Wait until I say “Go” to start.
Child makes a spontaneous correction
Do not discourage the child from making spontaneous self-corrections. If the child self-corrects responses to previously named quantities, the final response is recorded.
Child misnames two consecutive quantities in a single row of a test item
Immediately point to the second misnamed quantity and say, Keep going from here. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child skips a row or begins to complete a row in reverse order
Immediately point to the first box in the row to be completed and say, Keep going from here. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
Child hesitates at the end of a row
Say, Go on to the next row. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt. 
Child hesitates on a single quantity for more than 5 seconds
Say, Go on to the next one. Do not stop timing to provide this prompt.
