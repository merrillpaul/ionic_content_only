Instructions
Child asks for the name of a picture
Provide the name only.
Child’s name for a picture is unclear
Say, Show me.
Child does not select a picture in each row or selects more than one picture in a single row, provide the following prompts as often as necessary:
For two-row items, say, Pick one here (point across first row), and one here (point across second row).
 
For three-row items, say, Pick one here (point across first row), one here (point across second row), and one here (point across third row).
Child self-corrects
Say, You (touched, pointed to, said) [insert child’s response], and you (touched, pointed to, said) [insert child’s response]. Which one did you mean?
