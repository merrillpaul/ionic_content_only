Point to the sample item and say, Now you do these. Touch each animal. Do not touch anything else. Go ahead. Allow the child to complete the sample item.

Correct Incorrect

Correct response [marks the eight targets only]: Say, That’s right. Now you know how to do them.

Incorrect response(s) [omits a target or touches a non-target]:

Correct the error(s) as follows:

If the child omits a target, say, That’s not quite right (point to error). This is an animal, so you should touch it. Touch the omitted animal to highlight it.

If the child marks a non-target, say, That’s not quite right (point to error). This is not an animal, so you should not mark it. Touch the non-target again to remove the gray circle.

When the child has successfully completed the sample item, swipe to the next page.
