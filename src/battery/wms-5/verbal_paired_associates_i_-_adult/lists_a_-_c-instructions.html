Say, I am going to say a word and then say another word that goes with it. Some word pairs will make sense, like sock and shoe, but others may not make sense, like laugh and star. I will say a whole list of word pairs. Listen carefully because when I am finished, I will say the first word of each pair and ask you to tell me the word that goes with it. For example, if I say the word sock, you would answer (pause) shoe and if I say the word laugh you would answer (pause) star. Do you understand?

If the examinee does not understand, you may repeat the directions, paraphrasing when necessary.

When you are sure the examinee understands the directions, proceed to List A.
