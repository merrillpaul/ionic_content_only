Place the Memory Grid in front of the examinee with the word EXAMINEE closest to him or her. You should be able to read the word EXAMINEE in the correct orientation. No cards should be in the Memory Grid at the beginning of the item.

Present all of the cards to the examinee for each item administered. Always present the cards in a stack circle-side up.

Make sure that each card is securely in place, then lift the edge of the grid closest to you so you can see the card identifiers through the holes in the back fo the grid. The word TOP should appear at the top of the gird when lifted correctly.
