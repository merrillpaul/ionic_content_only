Materials

Client Device (Flat)
 

Administration

Start
Ages 16-69: Demonstration Items, Sample Item A, Sample Item B, then Item 5
Ages 70-90: Demonstration Items, Sample Item A, then Item 1

Reverse
Ages 16-69: If the examinee obtains a score of 0 on either Item 5 or Item 6, reverse until two consecutive scores of 1 are obtained, then proceed forward.

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0

Timing

Present each item stimulus for 5 seconds.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Prompt (P): Used to record when a subtest-specific prompt is provided

Repetition (R): Used to record when the examinee indicates a need for repetition

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point for each response.

Refer to the WMS 5 Digital Pilot Manual for additional information.
