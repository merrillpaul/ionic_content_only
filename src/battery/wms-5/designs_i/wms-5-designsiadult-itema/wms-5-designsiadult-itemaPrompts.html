Reorient the examinee to the screen if he or she does not appear to be attending to it. Say, Look at the screen so you can remember the designs and where they are on the screen.

When the screen clears, shuffle Cards A1-A8 and place them in a stack in front of the examinee. Say, Pick the four designs you saw from these cards. Put the cards in the same places here (point to the grid) as you saw them on the screen. Do not remove the cards when you are done.

Once the examinee's responses have been recorded, remove the cards from the Memory Grid and put the cards away. Place the empty grid in front of the examinee with the word EXAMINEE closest to him or her.
