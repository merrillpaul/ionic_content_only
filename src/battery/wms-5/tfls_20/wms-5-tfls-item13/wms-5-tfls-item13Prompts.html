Select Online Banking before administering the item.   

Place Card 1: Water Bill face up in front of the examinee and say, Pretend this is your water bill (pause to allow examinee time to look at bill), and this is a bank you can use to pay bills online (point to Bill Pay Center).  Show me how you would pay your water bill online (point to Water Bill).

 

If the examinee is unable to select ‘Pay a Company’,  navigate to the ‘Add a Company: Enter Information’ page and say, Now you enter the information. Record……

 

If the examinee asked what the deliver by date is, say, Pay the bill today.

If the examinee is unable to select ‘Continue’, navigate to the ‘Review Company and Payment Information’ page and say, Now you make a selection.’ Record……

 

If the examinee is unable to select ‘Make Payment’, complete the payment for them.

Record the examinee's response if any part of his/her response differ from example responses.
