Timing

For the majority of the TFLS 2.0 items, there are no time limits. As a general guideline, allow the examinee a maximum of two minutes to complete each task.

Item 11 should be administered 5 minutes after Item 6.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Prompt (P): Used to record when a subtest-specific prompt is provided

Scoring

Do not score.
