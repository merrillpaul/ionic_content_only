Materials

Client Device (Flat)

Administration

Start
Sample Item, then Item 1

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive imperfect scores
Note. An imperfect score is a score of 0 on Items 1 & 2, or a score of 0 or 1 on Items 3–26.

Never administer Symbol Span between Visual Reproduction I and Visual Reproduction II.

Timing

Present each item stimulus for 5 seconds.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Prompt (P): Used to record when a subtest-specific prompt is provided

Self-Correct (SC): Used to record when the examinee self-corrects. 

 

Scoring

Sample: If the sample is re-administered, delete the examinee's responses prior to re-administration.

Items 1-26: Ensure examinee's responses are in the correct order in the response section at the bottom of the screen. 

Score 0 points if examinee doesn't know (DK) or has no response (NR). 

Refer to the WMS 5 Digital Pilot Manual for additional information.
