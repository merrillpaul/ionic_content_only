Timing

For the majority of the TFLS items, there are no time limits. Although there are no set time limits, a maximum of two minutes per item is recommended. However, if at any point before two minutes have expired, it becomes clear that the examinee will not be able to complete an item, discontinue administration of the item.    

For Items 5 and 6, allow more than two minutes to complete the task if needed. 

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Prompt (P): Used to record when a item-specific prompt is provided
