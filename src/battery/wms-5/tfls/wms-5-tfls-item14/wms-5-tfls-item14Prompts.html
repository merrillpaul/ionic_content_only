Select Online Shopping before administering the item.   

Place the TFLS Express Card face up in front of examinee and say, Pretend this is your credit card and you are going to shop at this online store (point to online store). Show me how you would use the credit card to pay for a shirt from this store and have the shirt shipped to your home.

If the examinee repeatedly attempts to select a category other than Clothing or appears to be browsing the site, say, You are only buying a shirt.

 

If the examinee is unable navigate to the shirt selection page, navigate to the page and say, Now you select a shirt. Record by pressing PF

 

If the examinee is unable to select a shirt, choose any shirt and say, Now you complete the purchase.  Record by pressing PR

 

If the examinee is unable to proceed to the Checkout page, navigate to the page and say, Now you finish.  Record by pressing P

 

If the examinee asks if they should use their name, address, and phone number for the shipping information, say, Yes.

The examinee has two opportunities to enter the correct credit card information.  On the second attempt, the review pop up will appear regardless of the accuracy of the credit card information. Record...

 

If the examinee is unable to select ‘Place your order,’ complete the order for them.  Record by pressing PPS

Select Save & Exit to save a copy of the responses provided by the examinee and return to the Texas Functional Living Scale 2.0 start page.
