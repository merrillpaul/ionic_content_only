Before administering the item, sound the timer.

Once the timer sounds, the examinee should write down the name of the item bought online on the paper (e.g. Shirt).

If the examinee completes the task without prompt, record NP.

If the examinee does does not respond when the timer sounds, stop it after 10 seconds and say, Earlier I asked you to do something when the timer rings. What did I ask you to do? record OP.

If the examinee does not respond after receiving the oral prompt, point to the paper. Record PP.

If the examinee does not complete the task, record DK or NR as appropriate. Do not give any further prompts or cues.
