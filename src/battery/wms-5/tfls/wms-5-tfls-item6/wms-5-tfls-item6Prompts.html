Transition to the TFLS app on the Client Device, and select Online Banking at the start page. Place Card 1. Water Bill face up in front of the examinee and say, Pretend this is your water bill (pause to allow examinee time to look at bill), and this is a bank you can use to pay bills online (point to Bill Pay Center). Show me how you would pay your water bill online (point to Water Bill).

When the item is complete, select Save to save a copy of the responses or Exit to return to the start page.

Prompts

  • If the examinee is unable to select Pay a Company, navigate to the Add a Company: Enter Information page and say, Now you enter the information. Select Pay Company to indicate the prompt was given.
  • If the examinee is unable to select Continue after entering the information, navigate to the Review Company and Payment Information pop-up and say, Now you make a selection. Select Continue to indicate the prompt was given.
  • If the examinee asks what the deliver by date is, say, Pay the bill today.
  • If the examinee is unable to select Make Payment on the Review Company and Payment Information pop-up, complete the payment for them. Select Make Payment to indicate the prompt was given.
