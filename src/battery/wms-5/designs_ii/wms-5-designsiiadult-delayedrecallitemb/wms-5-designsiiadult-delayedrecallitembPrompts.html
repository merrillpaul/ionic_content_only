Shuffle Cards B1-B12 and place them in a stack in front of the examinee. Say, Earlier I showed you a screen with six designs. Pick the designs you saw from these cards. Put the cards in the same places here (point to the grid) as you saw them on the screen. Do not remove the cards when you are done.

Once the examinee's responses have been recorded, remove the cards from the Memory Grid and put the cards away. Place the Memory Grid in front of the examinee with the word EXAMINEE closest to him or her.
