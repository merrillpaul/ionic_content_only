Materials 

Client Device (Flat)
Response Booklet
Pencil with no eraser

Administration

Start
Item 1

Discontinue
Do not discontinue

Timing

Verbal Production (Item 12): Allow 30 seconds.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Prompt (P): Used to record when a subtest-specific prompt is provided

Repetition (R): Used to record when the examinee indicates a need for repetition

Self-correction (SC): Used to record when the examinee self-corrects on Items 7 & 8

Subvocalization (SV): Used to record when examinee subvocalizes

Scoring

Orientation (Items 1–5): Mark 1 point for correct answers and 0 points for incorrect answers.

Time Estimation (Item 6): Record the examinee's response and the actual time. Do not score.

Naming Stimuli 1–4: No scoring required.

Mental Control (Items 7 & 8): Record errors and self-corrections, but do not score. Record the completion time for each item.

Clock Drawing (Item 9): Score based on sequence of clock drawing (0, 1, or 2 points).

Incidental Recall (Item 10): Mark 1 point for correct answers and 0 points for incorrect answers.

Inhibition (Item 11): Record any skipped shapes (omission) and incorrectly named shapes (commissions). Include self-corrections as errors. Record the completion time for Item 11.

Verbal Production (Item 12): Record examinee's responses verbatim. Mark each response as correct, incorrect, or repeated.

Refer to the WMS 5 Digital Pilot Manual for additional information.
