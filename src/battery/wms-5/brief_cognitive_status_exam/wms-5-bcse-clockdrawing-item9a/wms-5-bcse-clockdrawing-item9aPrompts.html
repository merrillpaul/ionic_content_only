Open the Response Booklet to the page marked Brief Cognitive Status Exam — Item 9 and place the Response Booklet and a pencil with no eraser in front of the examinee.

You must observe the examinee as he or she draws in order to record appropriately.

Say, Draw the face of a clock, put in the numbers, and set the hands to 10 minutes after nine.
 
Do not allow the examinee to erase or restart the drawing. If the examinee attempts to erase or restart, say, Do not start over. Just do your best.
