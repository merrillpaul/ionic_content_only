Place the page marked Visual Reproduction I – Item 1, Trial 1 in the Response Booklet in front of the examinee so that the words Visual Reproduction I – Item 1, Trial 1 face the examinee.

Say, I will show you some designs. You will see and draw each design two times. Each time I show you a design, you will have 10 seconds to look at it. When it goes away, you will draw it here (point to the Response Booklet) from memory. Do not begin to draw until I tell you to. Ready?

​Present Item 1 stimulus for 10 seconds. Do not allow the examinee to begin drawing while the design is exposed.

​

Hand the examinee the pencil and say, Now draw the design. Begin timing.

The examinee may erase and make corrections. 

Stop timing after the examinee has finished drawing.
