Say, I will say a word and then say another word that goes with it. Some word pairs will make sense, like sock and shoe, but others may not make sense, like laugh and stand. I will say a whole list of word pairs. Listen carefully because when I am finished, I will say the first word of each pair and ask you to tell me the word that goes with it. For example, if I say the word sock, you would answer (pause) shoe and if I say the word laugh you would answer (pause) stand. Do you understand?

If the examinee does not understand, you may repeat the directions.
