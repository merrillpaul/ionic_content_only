Instructions

Examinee says, “I can’t draw” or is reluctant to try
Provide general encouragement by saying, Don’t worry about your artistic ability; just draw it as best you can or similar words.  

Examinee says, “I don’t remember”
Say, Well, just draw it as well as you remember or similar words.

Examinee refuses to respond to an item or hesitates while responding
Say, Just try it once more or Try it just a little longer or I think you can do it. Where useful, you may repeat a question after giving encouragement.

Examinee asks for help
Say, I want to see how well you can do it yourself.

Examinee requests repetition or does not appear to understand
Questions or instructions may be repeated.

DELAYED RECALL

Examinee does not respond or continues to hesitate after a prompt
Say, Let’s go on. Score zero on remaining item(s). Remove the Response Booklet and pencils, and proceed to Recognition.
