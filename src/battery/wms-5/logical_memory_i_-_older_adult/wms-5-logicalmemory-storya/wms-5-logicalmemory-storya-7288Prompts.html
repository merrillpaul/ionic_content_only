Say, I am going to read a short story to you. Listen carefully and try to remember it just the way I say it, as close to the same words as you can remember. When I am through, I want you to tell me everything I read to you. You should tell me all you can remember even if you are not sure. Ready?

Ruth and Paul have been friends for thirty years. They meet every Tuesday at Alma's Diner for breakfast and then they go for a walk in Mason Park.

After reading the story, say, Tell me everything you can remember about this story. Start at the beginning. Record or score as the examinee recalls the story.
