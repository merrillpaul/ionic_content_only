Administration

Start
Delayed Recall Item 1

Discontinue
Do not discontinue.

Timing

Administer 10–30 minutes after Verbal Paired Associates I.

Delayed Recall: Allow only 10 seconds for the examinee to respond to each item.

Recognition: Say the words in each pair about 1 second apart.

Recording

Don't Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of "I don't know" as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond

Prompt (P): Used to record when a subtest-specific prompt is provided

Repetition (R): Used to record when the examinee indicates a need for repetition

Scoring

Delayed Recall 
Select Correct or Incorrect for each response.

Recognition 
Select Yes or No for each response.

Word Recall
Correct: Any word that appeared in the original word list.

Repetition: Any correct or incorrect response that is repeated within the task.

Incorrect: Any word that did not appear in the original word list.

Refer to the WMS 5 Digital Pilot Manual for additional information.
